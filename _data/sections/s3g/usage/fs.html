S3G uses a folder structure similar to Jekyll. The site is generated from the src folder and outputted to the _site folder. An example is shown below

MyWebsite/
|--_data/
|  |--site.json
|  |--fonts/
|    |--roboto.html
|
|--_plugins/
|  |--MyPlugin.py
|
|--_s3g/
|
|--_templates/
|  |--navbar.html
|  |--share-button.html
|
|--src/
|  |--index.html
|  |--myPage.html
|
|--_site/
|  |--index.html
|  |--myPage.html
|
|--start.py
